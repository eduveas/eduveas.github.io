Classroom Lab

Human-Centered Computing @ Graz University of Technology

Mission & Vision

The Classroom Lab is dedicated to exploring and shaping the future of learning through cutting-edge technology and innovative didactic methods. By integrating state-of-the-art sensor and computing technologies, the lab enables students, educators, and researchers to experiment with novel approaches to enhance learning experiences. Our mission is to empower learners, support educators, and advance the scientific understanding of technology-supported education.

Key Research Areas

Adaptive and Immersive Learning Environments

Investigating AI-driven adaptive learning systems that personalize content and feedback. Exploring VR/AR-enhanced learning experiences to increase engagement and understanding.

Sensor-Based Learning Analytics and Interaction

Utilizing physiological and behavioral sensors to understand cognitive load, attention, and engagement. Developing real-time feedback systems for educators to optimize classroom interactions.
